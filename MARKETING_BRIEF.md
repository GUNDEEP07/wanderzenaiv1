> **WanderZenAI** is your personal AI travel planner that creates personalized, multi-day itineraries in 2 minutes. Tell us your destination, budget, and travel style—we'll generate a detailed itinerary with flight options, hotel recommendations, and authentic experiences tailored to you. Get a beautiful PDF itinerary delivered to your email.

## Why It Matters

### The Problem We Solve
Travel planning is broken:
- 😫 **Hours of research** - Comparing flights, hotels, activities across 10+ websites
- 😕 **Decision fatigue** - Overwhelmed by options, unsure what actually matches your style
- 💸 **Budget uncertainty** - Hidden costs only revealed after booking
- 🤖 **Generic suggestions** - TripAdvisor's "Top 10" visited by millions
- ⏰ **Time wasting** - Planning takes longer than the trip itself

### The WanderZenAI Solution
- ✅ **2-minute itinerary generation** - Vs. 6-8 hours of manual planning
- ✅ **Fully personalized** - AI learns your travel style and interests
- ✅ **Budget transparency** - See exactly what flights, hotels, and activities cost
- ✅ **Authentic experiences** - Beyond guidebooks, tailored to your preferences
- ✅ **Professional delivery** - Beautiful, shareable PDF itinerary

## FAQ for Marketing

**Q: What if I've already booked flights & hotels?**
A: Use our new "Activities Only" mode! Skip the logistics and jump straight to curating activities and experiences.

**Q: How accurate are the prices?**
A: We pull real data from travel APIs. Prices are estimates and vary by date/season, but give you a realistic budget range.

**Q: Can I edit the itinerary after downloading?**
A: Absolutely! The PDF is just a starting point. Modify, skip activities, rearrange days—it's yours to customize.

**Q: What if I don't like the suggestions?**
A: Adjust your interests, travel style, or budget and generate a new one. No limits on free tier users (1/month).

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes. We use your email only to send the itinerary PDF. No selling user data. Privacy policy on site.
