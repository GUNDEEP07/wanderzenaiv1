B — Refined card, stronger hierarchy

Keeps the card approach but with a stronger header, inline 3-col fields (days + date + who), emoji style grid instead of text pills. Everything on one focused page. Clean, modern, no wasted space.

Both directions share these fixes

  • Destination + duration + start date + who + travel style all on Step 1 (no more hunting across steps)
  • Step 2 (Venues) removes the duplicate header, breaks out of the narrow 680px card
  • Step 3 (Budget) gets a proper budget range slider or visual selector instead of a bare text input
  • Step 4 (Travel style / pace) becomes much shorter now that style is on Step 1
  • Progress bar becomes a slim 2px segmented bar (not big numbered circles)
